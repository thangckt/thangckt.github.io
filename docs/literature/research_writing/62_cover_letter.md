## Postdoc cover letter

1. list name and contact information: name, address, phone, email
2. the recipient's name, the university or company's name and their address
3. Write your introduction

    - explain why you are writing the cover letter in the begining paragraph
    - Refer to the position you're applying to and explain where you heard about the opportunity.
    - brief description of yourself and providing details about your academic journey.
    - Mentioning your dissertation topic, your field of study and your previous educational experience

4. Explain your qualifications
In the main body of your cover letter, explain why you are the right candidate for the position.

    - You can highlight your qualifications and elaborate on the skills that make you a good fit for the opportunity. 
    - **Build your content around the job posting** rather than keeping it generic.
    - remember to relate your research and findings back to the position. 
    - Explain how your work can benefit the institution you're applying to.

5. Conclude your letter

    - In the conclusion of your letter, explain where potential employers can find your CV or other relevant documents they might need to move forward with you in the hiring process.
    - Write something to thank your reader for their time and say you look forward to hearing from them soon.
